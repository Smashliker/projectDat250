Who are we?

We at AntiRepost are commited to the exchange of new ideas, and our site's purpose is therefore to hinder people copying other people's ideas and works.

We accomplish this by comparing the body of any new post with a selection of previous posts, and then disallow it from being posted if it is a certain percentage alike.
